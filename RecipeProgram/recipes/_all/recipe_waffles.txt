Name: [Waffles]

Tags: [[dairy], [eggs], [chocolate]]

Ingredients:
[2 c. Flour
1/2 c. Choc. Chips
1 tbsp. Sugar
1 tbsp. Baking Powder
1/2 tsp. Salt
1/2 tsp. Baking Soda
2  Eggs
1 3/4 c. Powdered Milk
1/4 c. Oil] 

Instructions: 
[Combine solids and liquids separately, then mix them together and use a 1/2 cup measure for the waffle maker, should make approx. 10 waffles.]
