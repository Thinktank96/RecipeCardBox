Name: [Chocolate Chunk Cookies]

Tags: [[dairy], [eggs]]

Ingredients:
[1 1/2 c. butter
1  c. sugar
2/3 c. brown sugar
2    eggs
2  tsp. vanilla
3  c. flour
1 1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
2  c. chips] 

Instructions: 
[Beat butter, sugar, and brown, sugar in a large bowl with electric mixer on medium speed until light and fluffy. Add eggs and vanilla, beat until well blended. Gradually add flour mixture, meating until well blended. Stir in choc. chips. drop heaping tbsp. of dough. Bake 11-13 min or until lightly browned. ]
