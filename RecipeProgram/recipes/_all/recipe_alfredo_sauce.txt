Name: [Alfredo Sauce]

Tags: [[sauce], [italian], [cream]]

Ingredients:
[1/2 c. Butter
1 1/2 c. Heavy Whipping Cream
2 tsp. Minced Garlic
1/2 tsp. Italian Seasoning
1/2 tsp. Salt
1/4 tsp. Pepper
2 c. Freshly Grated Parmesan Cheese] 

Instructions: 
[Add the butter and cream to a large skillet, simmer over low heat for 2 minutes. 

Whisk in the garlic, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper for one minute.

Whisk in the parmesan cheese until melted, then serve immediately.]
