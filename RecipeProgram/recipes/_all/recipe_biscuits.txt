Name: [Biscuits]

Tags: [[dairy], [eggs]]

Ingredients:
[3 c. Flour
2 1/2 tbsp. Sugar
4 1/2 tsp. Baking Powder
3/4 tsp. Salt
3/4 tsp. Cream of Tartar
3/4 c. Butter or Marg.
3/4 c. Milk
1  Egg] 

Instructions: 
[Sift together into a bowl, flour, sugar, b.powder, salt, and cream of tartar. Add butter or marg. with a fork or pastry blender. Blender mixture unitl it resembles coarse meal, then stir in egg and milk.
turn dough on a lightly floured surface, knead lightly for 4 or 5 strokes. Roll or pat into a 1/2-3/4 inch thick round. Cut with a 2 in biscuit cutter. Place on baking sheet and bake in a preheated 425 degree oven for 10-15 min.]
