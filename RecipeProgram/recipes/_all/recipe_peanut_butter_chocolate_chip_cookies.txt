Name: [Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies]

Tags: [[dairy], [eggs], [chocolate], [peanut butter]]

Ingredients:
[1/2 c. Butter
1/2 c. sugar
1/3 c. brown sugar
1/2 c. chunky peanut butter
1  egg
1 tsp. vanilla
1 c. flour
1/2 c. oatmeal
1 tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp. salt
1 c. choc. chips] 

Instructions: 
[Cream butter and sugars; beat in peanut butter, egg, and vanilla. Combine flour, oats, soda and salt; stir into the creamed mixture. Stir in choc. chips. Drop by rounded Tbsp. onto ungreased baking sheets. Bake at 350 degrees for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown. Cool 1 min. before removing to wire rack. Makes 2 dozen.]
