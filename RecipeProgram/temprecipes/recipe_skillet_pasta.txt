Name: [Skillet Pasta]

Tags: [[pasta], [spaghetti], [beef], [meat], [cheese]]

Ingredients:
[1 box 12oz. Pasta
1 lb. gr. beef, browned
4 c. 2 tsp. spaghetti sauce
1 c. Mozzarella cheese
1/2 c. sour cream
1/2 c. cream cheese, softened
1/2 c. Parmesan cheese] 

Instructions: 
[Cook pasta. Brown meat, set aside. Combine 1/2 Mozz. cheese, sour cream, cream cheese, & Parm. cheese. Add Spagh. sauce to meat. Add cheese mixture, stir lightly. Add Pasta, stir to combine, sprinkle with remaining 1/2 c. Mozz. cheese, cover to melt cheese.]
